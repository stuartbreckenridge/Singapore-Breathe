There are two APIs that are required for: Polluntant Standards Index (PSI) and PM2.5. No authentication is needed.

## PSI Bands

- Good (0 - 50)
- Moderate (51 - 100)
- Unhealthy (101 - 200)
- Very Unhealthy (201 - 300)
- Hazardous (>= 301)

## PM2.5 Bands

- Band 1 (NORMAL) - 0-55
- Band 2 (ELEVATED) - 56 - 150
- Band 3 (HIGH) - 151 - 250
- Band 4 (VERY HIGH) - >= 251
